Our Models

A7 Sportback TFSI e

A combination of full-size coupé aesthetics and innovative plug-in hybrid. The A7 Sportback TFSI e turns heads wherever it goes with an official test value of 38 - 41 miles of fully electric range.

e-tron GT quattro

The e-tron GT is built alongside the legendary Audi R8 at the Böllinger Höfe facility in Germany where attention to detail meets precision and quality. Enjoy an official test value range of up to 293 - 297 miles in this modern-day demonstration of progress, exclusivity and comfort.

Q8 Sportback e-tron

The fully-electric Audi Q8 Sportback e-tron has the unmistakable dimensions of an SUV with the dynamic silhouette of a sporty coupé - it also has an official test value range of up to 343 miles.
